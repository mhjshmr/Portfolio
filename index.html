Projects.




HERCULES GYM GEAR



The Hercules Gym Gear website, developed as part of a project associated with the University of West London - RAK Branch, offers a user-friendly platform dedicated to high-quality gym equipment. It features easy navigation for quick access to products, seamless checkout for hassle-free purchasing, and a responsive design that works on any device. The website showcases a diverse range of gym gear and PC parts, with regular updates on the latest products and exclusive special offers. With a focus on simplicity and strength, the website aims to make fitness journeys easier and more enjoyable. Additional features include customer support for product inquiries, a secure online payment system, and the ability to track orders. This project involved web design and development, highlighting the skills in creating a seamless online shopping experience.


NEW LIFE HOSPITAL



The New Life Hospital Management System, developed in collaboration with TCR Innovation and the University of West London - RAK Branch, is a cutting-edge platform designed to streamline healthcare services. Powered by Python, the system offers a reliable and efficient healthcare experience, including a user-friendly appointment booking feature that allows patients to schedule visits online with ease. The website's design, built using HTML and CSS, ensures a visually appealing and seamless navigation experience. This system aims to enhance the medical care and administrative processes for both patients and healthcare professionals. The project was guided by instructors Dr. Muhammed Ashad, Rutuja Doiphode, and Saheel Ramji.
